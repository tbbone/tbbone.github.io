About Us

Biswa Das:

I am the owner of a boutique consulting business (Cuboid Consulting Limited) in London specialising in providing technology support with regards to Financial Reporting, MI, and Big Data to the Big 4 accounting firms for clients across Financial Services, Media, Publishing, Pharma and local government. I am a blockchain advocate and have been following this space for numerous years.

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate and have over 16 years experience in technology and consulting domain working for organisations such as HP, IBM and Capgemini E&Y. I am part of the London funding circle investing in Fintech startups based out of London.

My previous experience also includes working with internet based startups and was a co-founder of ADDR.com, one of the earliest cloud hosting company based out of San Jose, US and Bangalore, India. During the late 1990s, I was an active technolgy contributor to CGI/Perl based open source communication platforms and was freelancing for web portals such as Rediff.com to develop one of the first web based chatting application. I also contributed heavily to Microsoft Visual Basic community and was one of the first developers in the world to work on C# back in 2000.

Currently, I advise companies on blockchain adoption and actively working on TBB for over 6 months.

Can Soysal (a.k.a. Jian Jolly):

Founder and managing director of Bitkapital, and an independent professional investor for over four years.

It was my dream to make blockchain technology more useful for capital market players, and with TBB we have come up with the structural and legal foundation of how to realise that vision. I am a computational physicist, also holding an MBA.

Nathan Sharp (a.k.a. wolfofwaves/trytostopme):

Founder of SHA256 LLC. Director at BitKapital Bitkapital.

I have a background in Information Technology with a focus in Networking.I am a CCNA and have a very keen mind for business, one of my specialties is networking and also I am A+ certified.
